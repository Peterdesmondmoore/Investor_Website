Platinum Market Analyst BETA

We convert platinum market complexity into structured, decision-grade insight

Platinum is a hybrid metal. Its price sits between global growth, autocatalyst and industrial demand, palladium-substitution dynamics, precious-metals flows, USD and liquidity conditions, and market stress. Those forces do not always agree, so platinum interpretation depends on identifying when cyclical industrial demand is dominant and when macro liquidation or safe-haven forces take over.

The Platinum Market Analyst combines macro signals, platinum-specific positioning, planned SPLT.L proxy pricing, planned ETF-flow context, metals-relative analysis, research, and news compression into a unified workflow. This page mirrors the current repository state: implemented components are linked, and the incomplete components still being wired into the stack are shown in red.

Model Architecture

How macro data, platinum-specific inputs, planned SPLT.L proxy pricing, planned ETF-flow context, metals-relative analysis, and narrative compression combine to produce the Platinum Outlook while leaving unfinished workflow nodes visible in red.

1. Generate platinum signals

Convert macro, market, and platinum-specific data into quantitative signals across global growth, real rates, USD and liquidity conditions, autocatalyst demand, metals-relative positioning, ETF flows, and pricing confirmation.

2. Synthesis (not aggregation)

Preserve economic meaning by evaluating how industrial demand, precious-metals spillovers, substitution pressure, macro stress, positioning, and pricing forces interact, identifying alignment, conflict, and dominance rather than collapsing signals into a single composite.

3. Decision-grade outputs

Produce platinum regime views, turning-point signals, and forward-looking interpretation that links macro conditions, industrial demand, and cross-metal context to price direction, risk, and opportunity.
