Gold Market Analyst BETA

We convert gold market complexity into structured, decision-grade insight

Gold is not a single-factor asset. Its price reflects real rates, liquidity, USD direction, inflation credibility, positioning, and stress, and those forces do not always point the same way. Interpreting gold means identifying which macro regime is dominant and when safe-haven demand starts to override the rest.

The Gold Market Analyst combines macro signals, pricing diagnostics, ETF-flow context, positioning, research, and narrative compression into a single workflow. This explainer mirrors the current repo state: implemented components link through to live pages, while incomplete components stay visible in red so the unfinished parts of the stack are explicit.

Model Architecture

How data, signals, pricing layers, ETF-flow context, and narrative compression are structured to produce the Gold Outlook while exposing the unfinished pieces still required for a complete gold workflow.

1. Generate gold signals

Convert macro, market, and precious-metals data into quantitative signals across real rates, liquidity, USD dynamics, inflation, positioning, ETF flows, and stress.

2. Synthesis (not aggregation)

Preserve economic meaning by evaluating how monetary conditions, inflation credibility, safe-haven demand, and positioning forces interact, identifying alignment, conflict, and dominance rather than collapsing signals into a single composite.

3. Decision-grade outputs

Produce gold regime views, turning-point signals, and forward-looking interpretation that links macro conditions and safe-haven demand to price direction, risk, and opportunity.
