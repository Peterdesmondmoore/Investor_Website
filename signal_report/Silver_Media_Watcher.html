Weekly Narrative Sentiment (Contextual)

The past week for Silver was marked by significant positive momentum, with futures rallying to repeated new all-time highs, at one point surpassing $170. This upward trend was strongly supported by a weakening U.S. dollar, robust industrial demand expectations—particularly from manufacturing data and infrastructure growth—and a broader market "flight to safety" amidst geopolitical concerns. Mining-specific news also contributed positively, with a major producer announcing a strategic pivot to silver focus and new high-grade discoveries and resource estimates reported. However, the period concluded with a sharp negative turn, as silver prices experienced a significant plummet of up to 15% on some exchanges, indicating a rapid correction following the strong rallies. Operational risks in the mining sector, such as employee abductions at a Mexican silver mine, also presented adverse supply-side context.
