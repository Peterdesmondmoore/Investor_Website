Weekly Narrative Sentiment (Contextual)

Silver narratives this week were characterized by a strong "safety trade" resurgence, as geopolitical tensions in the Middle East and uncertainty regarding U.S. trade tariffs drove investors toward hard assets. Fundamental support remained robust, with reports highlighting a projected sixth consecutive annual supply deficit and increased demand from the Chinese market reopening. Industrial tailwinds, particularly from the solar manufacturing sector, provided a persistent backdrop for price appreciation early in the week. However, the narrative shifted toward the end of the period as a strengthening U.S. dollar and hawkish Federal Reserve commentary triggered a tactical pullback from monthly highs. Overall, the conflict between structural supply bottlenecks and macroeconomic interest rate pressure remains the primary driver of market sentiment.
