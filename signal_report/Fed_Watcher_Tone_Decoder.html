Speaker: Chair Powell | Date: December 10, 2025 | Source: Press Conference Transcript

  • Labour Market: ✓ Acknowledged a gradually cooling labor market with rising downside risks to employment and potentially negative job creation (1).
  • Inflation Concern: ✗ Noted inflation remains somewhat elevated, primarily due to tariff effects on goods prices, while disinflation continues in services; non-tariff inflation is close to 2% (1).
  • Policy Bias: The policy rate was lowered by 1/4 percentage point, bringing it within a range of neutral estimates, with a strong signal to "wait and see how the economy evolves" for future adjustments (1).
  • Risk Asymmetry: Described a "difficult balance" between upside risks to inflation and downside risks to employment, suggesting a balanced approach as policy nears neutral (1).
