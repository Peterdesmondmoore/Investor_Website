Gemini Summary

The market for cyclicals and metals has transitioned to a Neutral sentiment, as indicated by the latest Industrial Production Signal as of August 2025. This follows a prolonged "Bearish" phase throughout most of 2024. Industrial Production (INDPRO YoY) is showing modest growth at +0.84% (Aug 2025), which, while positive, falls short of the robust demand threshold. Concurrently, the Mining share of GDP (RIWG21222S YoY) continues its strong expansion at +39.66% (Aug 2025). For equities, particularly cyclicals, this suggests moderate industrial activity but lacks the strong demand needed for aggressive upside. For precious and industrial metals, the growing supply proxy could temper price increases despite positive demand.

Technical Summary: Technically, the signal has shifted from "Bearish" (defined by contracting industrial demand and rising mining share) to "Neutral." The primary driver of this shift is INDPRO_YoY turning positive, moving out of contraction territory observed in 2024. However, the strong positive YoY growth in the Mining share of GDP (>0%) prevents a "Bullish" classification, implying that while demand isn't weak, supply-side factors may remain prominent.
