Methodology Applied:

Key Dynamics:

  • Internal tension exists between the contracting Fed Balance Sheet (-2.36% YoY) and the liquidity-releasing RRP drain.
  • Conditional Invalidation: An RRP usage acceleration greater than 0.5% (3M) would flip the primary easing impulse to tightening.

Scenario Balance:

  • Base case dominant: Continued liquidity support via RRP depletion offsetting balance sheet contraction.

  • Downside residual: Reserve balances (WRESBAL) transition to Bearish if YoY growth lags significantly behind assets.
