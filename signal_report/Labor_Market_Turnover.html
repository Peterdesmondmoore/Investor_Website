Gemini Summary

State of the Market Summary:

  • The labor market is currently in a Bearish regime as of August 2025. The Turnover Differential stands at 0.8 percentage points.
  • This represents a notable decline from the 2.1 pp peak observed in March 2022.
  • The labor market shows extreme positioning towards loosening conditions, predominantly Bearish since September 2024.

Technical Summary:

  • The robust z-score for August 2025 is -0.84. This falls below the methodology's -0.50 threshold for a Bearish regime.
  • This negative z-score signals a significant stretch towards looser labor conditions. It reflects an unwind from previously tight market periods.
  • Crowding risk has dissipated, replaced by consistent signals of labor market softening.
