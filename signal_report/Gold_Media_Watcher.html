Weekly Narrative Sentiment (Contextual)

Gold's narrative this week was largely positive, characterized by futures prices reaching new all-time highs. This rally was primarily fueled by persistent geopolitical tensions, robust industrial demand, particularly from the AI and data center infrastructure sectors, and a softer U.S. dollar. Key demand centers like India showed enduring strength with strong investment buying and record ETF inflows, while China reported a rebound in December demand and continued central bank purchases. However, a temporary pullback occurred after softer-than-expected U.S. CPI data tempered inflation expectations, highlighting sensitivity to economic data. Towards the end of the week, easing geopolitical worries led to some price slippage and exerted pressure on gold miners. Discussions around new gold discoveries and comparative scarcity also added long-term supply considerations to the narrative.
