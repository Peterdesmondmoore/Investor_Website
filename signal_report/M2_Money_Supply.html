Gemini Summary

The market, as indicated by the M2 Money Supply, is currently in a Bullish regime as of October 1, 2025. This marks a significant shift from a prolonged bearish period observed through much of 2023 and early 2024 (e.g., M2SL_YoY at -4.69% on 2023-04-01). Recent data shows M2SL Year-over-Year growth at 4.65% and 3-Month momentum at 1.22%, both comfortably exceeding their respective Bullish thresholds (YoY > 6% or 3M > 1%). This increasing liquidity could be supportive for risk assets such as equities and industrial metals, and potentially precious metals.

Technical Summary: Technically, the M2 signal has demonstrated a consistent "Bullish" trend since April 2025, driven by robust short-term momentum (3-month change consistently above 1%) and improving annual growth (YoY > 2%). This sustained positive momentum in money supply typically signals a supportive environment for asset prices, suggesting continued upward pressure or stability for liquidity-sensitive assets.
