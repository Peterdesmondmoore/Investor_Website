  • Pro-cyclical positioning implies global growth optimism is fully priced (1).

  • EURO positioning is stretched but has not reached extreme crowding levels (3).
  • Tensions do not overturn the regime because high-weight commodity proxies define the prevailing fragility.

Scenario Balance:

  • Dominant scenario: Optimistic global growth is fully priced. Market sensitivity to growth catalysts is asymmetrical.
  • Primary upside risk: Surprise China stimulus triggering a short-covering rally in commodity proxies (1).
  • Primary downside risk: Long liquidation in crowded markets triggered by growth-cycle shocks (2).

What Would Change the Regime:

  • USD Index crossing above the 12-month moving average with positive momentum (7).
