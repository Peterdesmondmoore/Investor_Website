Weekly Supply & Demand Sentiment (Contextual)

Gold market news over the last week exhibited a decidedly positive sentiment towards the supply-demand balance, driven predominantly by robust demand-side narratives. Central bank buying continues as a structural tailwind, while escalating geopolitical tensions, global economic uncertainties, expectations of lower interest rates, and a weakening U.S. dollar are fueling a strong surge in investment demand across various channels. Concurrently, narratives around supply highlight existing constraints from declining ore grades, underinvestment in new projects, and potential geopolitical risks impacting mine output, which, alongside a few instances of new mine openings and expansions, contribute to an overall tightening balance.
