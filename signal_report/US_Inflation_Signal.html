Methodology Applied:

  • Regime mapping defines HOT as $z \ge 1.0$, NEUTRAL between -0.5 and 1.0, and COOL as $z \le -0.5$.

  • Input series are resampled to monthly end dates with a 2–3 month bounded forward-fill.
