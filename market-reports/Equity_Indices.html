Subject: Weekly State of the Market - All Asset Classes (2025-09-23)

Executive Summary

The Commitments of Traders (CoT) data reveals a market characterized by extreme divergence and high conviction in specific sectors. While precious metals, industrial metals, and several agricultural commodities show significant speculative bullish overextension, core equity indices and other key cyclicals are experiencing deep bearish sentiment. This bifurcated positioning highlights areas of potential mean reversion, alongside systemic risks arising from crowded trades and significant interest rate market dislocations. The overall environment suggests a fragile equilibrium, susceptible to sharp reversals.
