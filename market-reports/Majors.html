Currencies

Executive Summary

The Commitments of Traders (CoT) data reveals a divergent market sentiment across currency pairs. A strong 'risk-on' bias drives extreme speculative long positions in emerging market currencies (Mexican Peso, Brazilian Real, South African Rand) and short-term rate futures (EURO SHORT TERM RATE), signaling crowded trades vulnerable to reversal. Conversely, developed market currencies (Canadian Dollar, Australian Dollar) exhibit significant net-short positioning, indicating potential for contrarian rallies.
