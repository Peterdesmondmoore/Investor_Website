Currencies

Executive Summary

The latest Commitments of Traders (CoT) data for 2025-09-23 reveals a divergent market sentiment across major currencies and short-term rates. Strong bullish conviction persists in several emerging market currencies and Euro short-term rates, indicating a continued risk-on environment and dovish rate expectations. However, this is counterbalanced by extreme bearish positioning in the Canadian Dollar, signaling significant contrarian potential. The market exhibits both robust trending opportunities and crowded trades vulnerable to sharp reversals.
