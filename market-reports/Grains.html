Subject: Weekly State of the Market - Grains & Oilseeds (2025-09-09)

Grains & Oilseeds

Executive Summary

The latest Commitments of Traders (COT) data for Grains & Oilseeds indicates a general strengthening of bearish speculative sentiment across the complex. Speculators are actively increasing their net-short positions in key markets like Soybeans, Soybean Meal, Wheat-SRW, and Corn. Canola shows a significant flip from bullish to bearish. Commercial hedgers largely maintain their counter-trend positions, suggesting fundamental alignment against speculative fervor.

Key Opportunities

  • Rough Rice & HRW Wheat Short Covering: Speculators slightly reduced bearish bets, indicating potential for short-covering rallies if market fundamentals shift positively.
  • Canola Reversal: A sharp pivot to net-short positioning might present a tactical opportunity if underlying supply-demand dynamics prove more resilient than speculative sentiment suggests.

Key Risks

  • Soybean & Corn Downside Pressure: Speculators are aggressively adding to net-short positions, signaling strong conviction in continued price declines. Commercials remain net-short, reinforcing the bearish outlook.
  • Wheat Markets Sustained Bearishness: HRSpring and SRW Wheat show persistent and increasing speculative net-shorts, indicating entrenched negative sentiment that could drive further price erosion.
