Subject: Weekly State of the Market - Industrial Metals (2025-09-09)

Industrial Metals

Executive Summary

The latest Commitments of Traders (COT) data for Industrial Metals presents a bifurcated market sentiment. Copper exhibits robust speculative bullishness, signaling continued strength. Aluminum MWP shows budding bullish interest. Conversely, Steel-HRC, Lithium Hydroxide, and Cobalt remain under bearish speculative pressure, though some short covering activity is noted.

Key Opportunities

  • Copper's Sustained Uptrend: Significant increase in speculative net-long positions and rising open interest strongly supports potential for continued upward price momentum.
  • Aluminum MWP Building Momentum: Increased speculative net-longs suggest growing bullish sentiment and potential for further price appreciation.

Key Risks

  • Lithium & Cobalt Downside Pressure: Persistent and in some cases extreme net-short speculative positions indicate continued bearish sentiment, suggesting potential for further declines.
  • Steel-HRC Weakness: Speculators remain net-short and increased their bearish bets, indicating potential for ongoing price vulnerability.

Asset Class Summaries

COPPER- #1

  • Speculators: Dominantly bullish with a significant net-long position (+27,241 contracts), increasing bets by +1,583 WoW. Open Interest surged (+12,683). Positioning is strongly bullish (Z-score: 1.03).
  • Commercials: Heavily net-short (-36,015 contracts), increasing their short exposure WoW.
  • Implication: Strong speculative conviction and money flow underpin potential for continued price appreciation. Commercial hedging indicates increasing supply concerns or potential reversal risk at higher levels.

ALUMINUM MWP

  • Speculators: Net-long (+1,249 contracts), increasing bullish conviction by +175 WoW. Z-score is positive (0.53).
  • Commercials: Net-short (-1,173 contracts), slightly reducing short exposure WoW.

COBALT
