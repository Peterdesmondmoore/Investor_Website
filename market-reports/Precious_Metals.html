Subject: Weekly State of the Market - Precious Metals (2025-09-16)

Precious Metals

Executive Summary

The latest Commitments of Traders (COT) data for Precious Metals reveals a nuanced landscape. Gold and Silver remain under significant speculative bullish pressure, albeit with slight reductions in net-longs this week. Platinum shows a continued build-up in speculative bullish conviction from a more neutral base. Palladium presents a compelling short-covering scenario as speculators reduce extreme net-short positions.

Key Opportunities

  • Palladium Short Squeeze Setup: Managed Money reduced extreme net-short positioning, indicating potential for a significant short-covering rally given supportive Commercial long positioning.
  • Platinum Building Momentum: Speculators increased net-longs WoW from a neutral base, signaling strengthening bullish conviction and potential for continued upward price action.

Key Risks

  • Gold & Silver Long Exhaustion: Managed Money remains highly net-long (Gold Z-score: 2.085, Silver Z-score: 1.592), despite minor WoW reductions, posing a significant risk of sharp corrections if profit-taking accelerates or sentiment shifts.
  • Gold Bearish Divergence: Managed Money net-longs decreased WoW while Commercials further increased net-short positions, indicating a strong divergence that could signal an imminent market top.
