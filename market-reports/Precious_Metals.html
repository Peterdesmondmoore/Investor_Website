Precious Metals

Executive Summary

The Precious Metals market exhibits an overwhelmingly bullish sentiment, largely driven by significant speculative net-long positioning in Gold and Silver. This suggests a 'risk-on' environment for safe-haven or inflation-hedge assets. The most significant themes are the extreme speculative overextension in Gold and Silver, signaling potential exhaustion, and notable divergences in Gold and Palladium futures suggesting underlying market uncertainty.
