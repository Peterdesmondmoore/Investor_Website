ProShares UltraShort Silver is a leveraged inverse exchange-traded fund (ETF). It is designed for sophisticated traders with a bearish short-term outlook on silver.

The investment seeks daily investment results, before fees and expenses, that correspond to two times the inverse (-2x) of the daily performance of the Bloomberg Silver SubindexSM. This means if the price of silver falls by 1% on a given day, ZSL is designed to rise by approximately 2%, and vice versa.

ZSL does not hold any physical silver. It achieves its objective by using financial derivatives, such as futures contracts and swap agreements, to get short exposure to the silver market. Because it resets daily, its performance over longer periods can differ significantly from the stated -2x objective due to the effects of compounding.
