The WisdomTree Copper ETC (COPA) is an exchange-traded commodity designed to offer investors a simple and direct way to gain exposure to the price of copper. As an ETC, it trades on stock exchanges like a regular share, providing an alternative to the complexities of the futures market or purchasing and storing physical copper.

The investment seeks to track the Bloomberg Copper Subindex. The ETC is physically-backed, meaning the issuer holds corresponding physical copper metal in secure warehouses. This structure is designed to provide a return that is closely correlated with the spot price of copper, less the applicable fees.

Unlike investing in mining stocks, this ETC's value is tied directly to the underlying commodity, removing company-specific risks. And unlike futures-based funds, being physically-backed helps mitigate the potential negative impacts of contango that can affect futures contracts. Please note, this instrument is not listed on the ASX.
