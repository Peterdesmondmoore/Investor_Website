SPDR® Gold MiniShares Trust (GLDM) is an exchange-traded fund (ETF) designed to offer investors a cost-effective and convenient way to invest in gold.

The investment seeks to reflect the performance of the price of gold bullion, less the Trust's expenses. Each share of GLDM represents a fractional, undivided beneficial ownership interest in the Trust, whose only asset is physical gold bullion and, from time to time, cash.

GLDM is designed to provide investors with a lower-cost alternative to the larger SPDR® Gold Shares (GLD), with one share of GLDM initially representing 1/100th of an ounce of gold. The gold is held by the custodian, HSBC Bank plc, in its London vault, providing investors with direct exposure to physical gold without the need to store or insure it themselves.
