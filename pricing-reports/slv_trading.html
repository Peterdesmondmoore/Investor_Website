The iShares Silver Trust is the largest and most popular silver Exchange-Traded Fund (ETF) in the world.

To track the price of silver. The goal is simple: if the spot price of silver goes up by 1%, the value of an SLV share should also go up by approximately 1% (minus expenses). If the price of silver falls, SLV will fall with it.

SLV achieves its objective by holding physical silver bullion in secure, audited vaults (primarily in London and Zurich). When you buy a share of SLV, you are buying a fractional interest in the total silver held by the trust. The fund's manager, BlackRock, issues and redeems shares based on investor demand, physically buying or selling silver bars to ensure the ETF's price stays in line with the underlying metal's value.
