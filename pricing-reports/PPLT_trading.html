The abrdn Physical Platinum Shares ETF (PPLT) is an exchange-traded fund designed to provide investors with a straightforward and cost-effective way to gain exposure to the price of physical platinum. It trades on the stock exchange like a regular stock, making it accessible for a wide range of investors.

The investment seeks to reflect the performance of the price of platinum, less the Trust's expenses. To achieve this, the fund holds physical platinum bullion stored in secure vaults. Each share represents a fractional, undivided beneficial interest in the Trust's net assets, which consist primarily of physical platinum bars.

Unlike financial products that use futures contracts or other derivatives to simulate market exposure, PPLT is physically-backed. This means the value of the ETF is directly tied to the spot price of the platinum it holds. Investing in PPLT offers an alternative to the practical challenges of purchasing, storing, and insuring physical platinum bullion directly.
